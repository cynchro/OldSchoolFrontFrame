# Code of Conduct

We want participation in OlsSchoolFrontFrame to be respectful and constructive for everyone.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn.
- Stay on topic in issues and pull requests.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption
- Publishing others’ private information without permission

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, please contact the project maintainers by opening a private issue or, if available, using the contact method listed in the repository.

Reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or other behavior that does not align with this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project spaces.

---

This document is adapted from common open-source community norms and may evolve as the project grows.
